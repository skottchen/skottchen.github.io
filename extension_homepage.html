Thanks for visiting the homepage for the Import UMD Schedule to Google Calendar extension!
Download the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
The purpose of this extension is automatically import UMD (University of Maryland, College Park) students' course schedules from the university system into Google Calendar, saving them time that they would use to do it manually.
We do not collect or use any information from users.
